気象変化と不定愁訴との関連

This literature is about relation between weather change and indefinite complaint.
The experiment was conducted to clarify the relation of them. In the survey,
subjects were questioned some questions such as about condition on bad weather day.
About 40% of subjects answered that their condition are bad on bad weather day and
there are barely difference about sex. There are significant differences in a few
questions. For instance, the number of subjects who have many times having headache
in daily life, and have meteoropathy is significant larger than the number of subjects
who do not have meteoropathy. That experiment clarified that approximately half of subjects
have indefinite complaint by change weather. Some factors like sex, inner ear sensor do not have
impact on indefinite complaint
Reference: 東京有明医療大学 学術リポジトリ
