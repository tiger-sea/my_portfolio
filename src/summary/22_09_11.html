気圧や気温の変化が痛みに与える影響について

This literature is about impact of air pressure and temperature on body pain.
They investigated subjects' awareness of the topic, and analyzed relation
between body pain and air pressure•temperature. It was not found that
there is a significant difference as a result. In addition, in this research,
air pressure had the most impact on body pain.
